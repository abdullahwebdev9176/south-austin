WHY AVALON?

At Avalon, we are a fully vertically integrated builder, meaning the large majority of our pontoon boats—from pontoons and framing to furniture and more—is crafted right here in our own facility. This hands-on approach gives us unparalleled control over every aspect of production, ensuring the highest standards of quality at every step. Unlike competitors who rely on outsourcing, we oversee every detail in-house, allowing us to deliver boats with superior craftsmanship, consistency, and durability all backed by our limited lifetime structural warranty.
